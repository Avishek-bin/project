Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using ideatePad for your creative projects.

Is ideatePad free to use?

Yes! ideatePad is completely free for personal use. We also offer premium plans for teams and organizations with advanced features.

Can I collaborate with others in real-time?

Absolutely! ideatePad supports real-time collaboration. Simply share your board link and work together with your team instantly.

How do I save my drawings?

Your work is automatically saved. You can also export your creations as PNG, SVG, or ideatePad files to share or use in other applications.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to use basic features. However, signing up allows you to save boards, access them across devices, and collaborate with teams.

Can I use ideatePad offline?

Yes! ideatePad works offline once loaded. You can create and edit drawings without internet connection, perfect for presentations on the go.

Is my data secure?

We prioritize your privacy. Your drawings are encrypted, and we don't store or access your content unless you explicitly save it to our servers.
